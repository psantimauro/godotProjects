# Research

Research (Technologies) allows the player to unlock new capabilities and improve existing ones.

## Mechanics
- **Unlockable in Buildings**: Available research items are tied to specific [[Buildings]].
- **Cost**: Usually requires [[Resources]] to complete.
- **Trigger**: Research projects can be triggered via [[Quests]] or by reaching milestones.

## Types of Research
- **Job Unlocks**: Specifically makes a new job available in a building (e.g., "Unlock Trapping").
- **Job Improvements**: Improves the stats of an existing job (speed, yield).
- **Global Unlocks**: Unlocks new game systems like [[Trading]].

## Technical Details
- Base Script: `base_tech_resource.gd`
- Managed by: [[BuildingManager]]
